2.  合并PacBio_HiFi_CSS数据
```bash
cat ~/data/taishan01/PacBio_HiFi_CSS/cell_1/taishan01.ccs.fastq.gz \
    ~/data/taishan01/PacBio_HiFi_CSS/cell_1/taishan01.ccs.fastq.gz \
    ~/data/taishan01/PacBio_HiFi_CSS/cell_1/taishan01.ccs.fastq.gz \
    > ~/data/taishan01/PacBio_HiFi_CSS/taishan01.ccs.fastq.gz

### 运行hifiasm进行组装:
```bash
hifiasm \
    -o taishan01_hifiasm \
